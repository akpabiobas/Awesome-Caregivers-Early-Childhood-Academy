About Us

Our History

Awesome Caregivers Early Childhood Academy Kubwa Abuja Nigeria is an early childhood education centre, which cater to children from 6weeks . It is strongly believed that if childhood education is well laid, children will not grope in the dark in all spheres of life. And of course, the God factor is the anchor through Christ Jesus.

Founded in 2010, Awesome Caregivers Early Childhood Academy has been providing quality education and care for children in Kubwa, Abuja. We started with a vision to create a nurturing environment where learning is fun and rewarding.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Child-Centered Learning
  • Creativity and Innovation
  • Respect and Diversity
  • Community Partnership

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can learn, grow, and develop to their full potential.

Our Vision

To be the leading early childhood education center that fosters a love for learning and prepares children for future academic success.

Programs & Academics

Creche

Our creche provides a safe and nurturing environment for infants and toddlers with qualified caregivers and age-appropriate activities.

Pre-Nursery

Our pre-nursery program focuses on developing social, emotional, and cognitive skills through play-based learning activities.

Nursery

Our nursery program prepares children for primary education with a balanced curriculum that includes literacy, numeracy, and creative arts.

After-school Care

We provide a safe and supervised environment for children after school hours with homework assistance and engaging activities.

Tutorials

Specialized tutorial sessions are available to support children who need extra help in specific subjects or skill areas.

Extracurriculars

We offer a variety of extracurricular activities including sports, cultural programs, career orientation, and media education.

Admissions

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical records and immunization history
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Passport photographs

Enrollment Process

  1. Submit completed application form with required documents
  2. Attend an interview and assessment (if required)
  3. Receive admission decision
  4. Pay required fees
  5. Complete registration and receive welcome package

Facilities & Services

Cozy Creche

Our creche is designed to provide a home-like environment for our youngest learners with comfortable sleeping areas, play spaces, and feeding facilities.

Interactive Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with age-appropriate learning materials, educational technology, and creative spaces to stimulate young minds.

Conducive Environment

Our campus features safe, child-friendly playgrounds, green spaces, and modern facilities that create an ideal learning environment.

Daycare Services

We offer full-day daycare services with structured activities, nutritious meals, and rest periods for children of working parents.

After-school Care

Our after-school program provides a safe space for children to complete homework, engage in recreational activities, and socialize.

Experienced Teachers

Our team of qualified and experienced educators are passionate about early childhood development and dedicated to each child's success.
